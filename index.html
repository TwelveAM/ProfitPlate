Stop guessing. Know your dish cost.

ProfitPlate is a simple tool for restaurants to calculate exact cost per dish, portion, and profit margin — in seconds, not spreadsheets.

Ingredient-based costing

Add ingredients once, use them in any recipe. ProfitPlate calculates cost per portion automatically.

Live price updates

Update supplier prices and see every affected dish update instantly.

Clean PDF exports

Export dish costing sheets you can print, share with your team, or keep for documentation.

Built for real kitchens

Designed by a working chef, not an accountant. Simple enough to use during service.
