Stop guessing. Know your dish cost.

ProfitPlate is a simple tool for small restaurants to calculate real cost per dish, portion and profit margin — in seconds, not spreadsheets.

Ingredient-based costing

Add ingredients once, use them in any recipe. ProfitPlate calculates cost per portion automatically using your latest supplier prices.

Live price updates

Update supplier prices and see every affected dish update instantly. Perfect when butter, pancetta or salmon jump in price overnight.

Clean PDF exports

Export dish costing sheets you can print, share with your team or keep for documentation and menu engineering.

Built for real kitchens

Designed by a working chef, not an accountant. Simple enough to use during service, powerful enough for menu planning days.

How ProfitPlate fits in your day

A quick rhythm that matches how you actually work.

  1. Update your Purchases. Add or edit items from your latest invoices – butter, pancetta, fries, flour. Set category, sub-type, unit and price (or use the pack helper if the invoice is weird).
  2. Build your Recipes. Pick ingredients from Purchases, set the quantities and units you actually use in the dish. ProfitPlate calculates batch cost and cost per portion automatically.
  3. Check margin & export. Adjust selling price until the margin looks healthy. When you’re happy, export a clean PDF or keep it in ProfitPlate for later.
