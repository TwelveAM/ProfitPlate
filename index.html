How ProfitPlate fits in your day

1. Update your Purchases

In the morning, you add or update items in Purchases: butter, pancetta, salmon, fries, flour. Set category, sub-type, unit and price (or use the pack helper if the invoice is weird).

2. Build your Recipes

In Recipes, you pick ingredients from your Purchases list, set quantities and units actually used in the dish, and ProfitPlate calculates cost per portion and batch.

3. Check margin & export

Adjust selling price until the margin looks healthy. When you’re happy, export a clean PDF or just keep it in ProfitPlate for later.

ProfitPlate is in early development. This version runs entirely in your browser using local storage — no login, no cloud, just your device. In the future this will sync to an online account so you can share data between phone, tablet and office PC.
