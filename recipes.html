Recipes

See cost per portion and profit margin for each dish on your menu. Adjust selling price and keep an eye on how ingredient changes affect your bottom line.

Add a new recipe
Give the recipe a name, set how many portions it makes, then add ingredients from your Purchases. ProfitPlate will calculate cost per portion automatically.
Ingredients
Choose items from your Purchases list, set the quantity and unit actually used in this recipe. ProfitPlate will use the latest purchase price to compute the cost.
Menu overview
Cost automatically updates when ingredient prices change in Purchases. Recipe data is stored locally in your browser using ProfitPlate's tiny brain. Changing ingredient prices in Purchases will change costs here next time you open the page.
